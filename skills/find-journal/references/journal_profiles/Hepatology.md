## Scope
Hepatology publishes original research, reviews, and clinical studies on liver disease, including viral hepatitis, non-alcoholic fatty liver disease (NAFLD/MASLD), hepatocellular carcinoma, cirrhosis, liver transplantation, cholestatic diseases, and drug-induced liver injury. The journal covers basic science, translational research, and clinical hepatology.

## Special Notes
Hepatology is the flagship journal of AASLD and the premier venue for liver research. Imaging studies are published when they advance liver disease diagnosis, staging, or monitoring (e.g., LI-RADS validation, elastography, contrast-enhanced MRI). AI-based liver imaging studies should demonstrate clear clinical impact on hepatology practice. The journal has a companion publication, Hepatology Communications (OA).
