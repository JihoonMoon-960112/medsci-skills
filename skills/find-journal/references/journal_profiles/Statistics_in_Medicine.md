## Scope
Statistics in Medicine publishes original research on statistical methods and their application to medical research. Coverage includes clinical trial design, survival analysis, longitudinal data analysis, diagnostic test evaluation, meta-analysis methodology, causal inference, and missing data methods. The journal emphasizes methodological innovation motivated by real medical problems.

## Special Notes
Statistics in Medicine is the premier journal for new statistical methods in medicine. Papers should present novel methodology or substantial extensions of existing methods, with application to real clinical data. Purely applied papers without methodological novelty are better suited to clinical journals. The journal is particularly strong in meta-analysis methodology, diagnostic test statistics, and clinical trial design. Tutorials on statistical methods are valued.
