## Scope
Academic Medicine publishes research, reviews, and commentary on medical education, academic health care, and the medical profession. Coverage includes undergraduate and graduate medical education, continuing professional development, simulation-based training, assessment methods, curriculum design, faculty development, diversity and inclusion in medicine, and health care workforce issues.

## Special Notes
Academic Medicine is the flagship journal of the AAMC and the highest-impact medical education journal. It favors rigorous educational research with clearly described theoretical frameworks and validated outcome measures. AI in medical education studies (e.g., AI tutoring systems, LLM performance on board exams, AI-assisted assessment) are of growing interest. Innovation Reports allow description of novel educational programs without requiring full research methodology.
