## Scope
BMC Medical Research Methodology publishes original research on methodological approaches to medical and health research. Coverage includes study design, data collection, statistical analysis, systematic review methodology, qualitative methods, measurement science, and research reporting. The journal emphasizes practical methodological guidance applicable across medical disciplines.

## Special Notes
BMC Medical Research Methodology is fully open access and a strong venue for methodological papers that do not fit neatly into clinical journals. It is particularly receptive to systematic review methodology, development and validation of risk of bias tools, reporting guideline development, and simulation studies comparing statistical approaches. Study protocols for systematic reviews are commonly published here. The journal has a practical orientation, making methods accessible to applied researchers.
