## Scope
Annals of Surgery publishes original research on surgical science and practice across all surgical specialties, including general surgery, surgical oncology, transplant surgery, trauma, and minimally invasive surgery. The journal emphasizes studies that advance surgical technique, improve perioperative outcomes, or inform surgical decision-making through clinical trials, large cohort studies, and meta-analyses.

## Special Notes
Annals of Surgery is the oldest and highest-ranked general surgery journal. It values large multicenter surgical trials and practice-changing outcome studies. Imaging and AI studies are published when they directly impact surgical planning, intraoperative decision-making, or postoperative monitoring. Meta-analyses of surgical interventions are well-received. The journal has a rigorous statistical review process.
