## Scope
Gut publishes original research and reviews in gastroenterology and hepatology, covering the entire gastrointestinal tract from esophagus to anus, liver, biliary system, and pancreas. The journal emphasizes clinical and translational research with direct relevance to patient care, including endoscopy, motility, microbiome, inflammatory bowel disease, and GI oncology.

## Special Notes
Gut is the leading European gastroenterology journal and one of the top GI journals globally. It has published several landmark AI in endoscopy studies (polyp detection, Barrett esophagus). Radiology-related GI studies (CT colonography, MR enterography) are published when they address clinical GI questions. The journal values large multicenter studies and is particularly strong in IBD and GI oncology research.
