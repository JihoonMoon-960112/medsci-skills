## Scope
CHEST publishes original research, reviews, and guidelines in pulmonary medicine, critical care, and sleep medicine. Coverage includes chronic obstructive pulmonary disease, asthma, lung cancer, interstitial lung disease, pulmonary hypertension, venous thromboembolism, mechanical ventilation, and sleep-disordered breathing. The journal emphasizes clinical research with direct relevance to pulmonary and critical care practice.

## Special Notes
CHEST is the flagship journal of the American College of Chest Physicians (ACCP). Lung imaging studies (CT lung screening, lung nodule management, Lung-RADS) are core to the journal's scope. AI studies for lung nodule detection, COPD quantification, and chest CT interpretation are increasingly published. The journal is a key venue for Lung-RADS and Fleischner Society guideline-related research.
